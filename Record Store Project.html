About Purple Magic Records...

Local vinyl curation • Trade-ins welcome

Welcome to Purple Magic Record Store. As noted, we have served vinyl music lovers since 2020. Our store specializes in new and used vinyl records. We carry a wide selection of genres—rock, pop, jazz, classical, and more. We are located in downtown State College. Our store hours are Monday through Saturday from 10am to 7pm. Stop by and browse our collection. Our staff is knowledgeable and passionate about music. Whether it is classical or something more modern, we help find exactly what you are looking for. Plus, we buy used records. So, if you have a collection you want to sell, bring it in and allow us to make a reasonable offer.

Thank you for supporting local music stores and Penn State student entrepreneurship.
