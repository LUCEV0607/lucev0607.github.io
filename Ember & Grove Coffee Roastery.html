Our Story

Founded in 2018 by Laurence Lucy-Evans and Jacinda Walker, within a renovated warehouse, in Portland's Alberta Arts District; Ember & Grove began as a passion project.

The prevailing belief is that great coffee starts with relationships. That is the very reason why we travel directly to origin, and meet the farmers who dedicate their lives to cultivating exceptional beans. To promote the local economy, we routinely pay above fair-trade prices and maintain long-term partnerships. It remains imperative that these relationships are built on mutual respect and shared values.

Moreover, every bean and batch is roasted on our vintage 1970s Probat drum roaster. Still, because some things cannot be automated, by sight, sound, and smell; we monitor each roast. The result? Coffee that honors its origin, its region, and showcases our craft.

"Coffee should tell a story—of the farmer, the land, and the roaster. Jacinda and I are fortunate to be part of that narrative." - Laurence Lucy-Evans, Co-Founder
