Directions to Bella Vista

Address: 456 Italian Avenue, Little Italy District

From Downtown: Take Highway 90 East, exit at Italian Avenue (Exit 12). Turn right and our location is two blocks down on the left.

From Airport: Follow signs to Downtown, merge onto Highway 90 East. Take Exit 12 for Italian Avenue.

Parking: Free valet parking available in the evenings. Street parking during normal lunch hours.

About Us

Bella Vista has served authentic Italian cuisine since 1935. Our historic family recipes bring the true taste of Italy to your table. Plus, with classic cocktails and live jazz on weekends, our hidden speakeasy bar offers a unique and retro dining experience.
